Unified Command and Control Centre
Mumbai Division

Unified Command and Control Centre at Mumbai Division of Western Railway.It integrates information systems of all functions (Freight, Coaching, Commercial, Infra, Finance, HR, Procurement Medical, S&T, Engineering) of the Railways for optimal performance, including VA and FRS for the 2700+ CCTV network across the Mumbai Suburban as well.

The Unified Command and Control Centre (UCCC) was inaugurated by Shri. Suneet Sharma Chairman & CEO, Railway Board on his recent review visit to Western Railway on 7th November, 2021. Shri Suneet Sharma was very much impressed with the UCCC and was full of praise for this advanced technology.
